PROCESS

Discovery

1

During this phase I'll learn all about your project. I want to understand your needs and what you have envisioned so we're both on the same page. This is where I'll do my homework and gather everything I can about your business and customers.

Strategize

2

This is where the magic happens. I'll come up with an idea and solution to your problem. Together we'll agree on what work will be executed. Most of the time I'll work with sketches and moodboards to present these solutions.

Execute

3

At this point I'll begin the design and development work. Continual progress will be shared for ongoing feedback. This is an ongoing process where we work together until we are both happy.

Launch

4

I'll make any final requested changes to your project and flip the switch. Once your project is live you will be briefed with any necessary training required.
