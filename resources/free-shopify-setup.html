FAQS

Why are you doing this for free?

Once your free Shopify ecommerce store is setup you'll pay Shopify a monthly fee based on the plan you choose. From this monthly fee we're paid a commission from Shopify. We get paid and you get a Shopify ecommerce store setup for free.

Can my store look a certain way?

Yes. You'll have the opportunity to tell us exactly what you want. You can show us examples and give us a detailed breakdown to follow. We'll tell you what's possible and what would not be covered under our free setups.

Can I request changes and additional revisions?

Our free Shopify setup includes unlimited revisions while in development. Customizations that are not covered by our free setup service will be charged at $65/hr.
